About Us

Welcome to TigaDuaEnam, where every stitch is a testament to creativity and craftsmanship. At TigaDuaEnam, we believe in the beauty of the handmade, the allure of intricate beadwork, and the timeless elegance of a well-crafted accessory. Our beaded bags are more than just fashion statements. They are expressions of artistry, individuality, and passion.

Each piece in our collection is meticulously handcrafted by skilled artisans, using traditional techniques passed down through generations. From vibrant patterns to delicate detailing, every TigaDuaEnam bag tells a unique story, reflecting the rich cultural heritage and creativity that inspire us. Whether you're strolling through city streets or attending a special event, our beaded bags are designed to elevate your style and make a statement.

Explore our curated collection and experience the artistry of TigaDuaEnam, "Where craftsmanship meets beauty, and every bag is a work of art".


Our Vision

At TigaDuaEnam, we envision a world where handmade craftsmanship thrives, where the artistry of beaded bags becomes a symbol of cultural appreciation and individual expression. We aspire to be at the forefront of this movement, inspiring others to embrace the beauty of artisanal creations and celebrate the stories woven into each meticulously crafted piece.


Our Mission

Our mission at TigaDuaEnam is to preserve and promote the art of handmade beaded bags, honoring tradition while fostering innovation. We strive to empower talented artisans, providing them with sustainable livelihoods and opportunities to showcase their skills on a global stage. Through meticulous attention to detail, ethical sourcing practices, and a commitment to quality, we aim to deliver exquisite beaded bags that captivate the senses and evoke a sense of wonder. We are dedicated to enriching lives through the beauty of craftsmanship, making a meaningful impact on individuals, communities, and the world we inhabit.
